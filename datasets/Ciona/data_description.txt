The source data for the central nervous system (CNS) connectome of Ciona intestinalis can be accessed from the publication "Ryan K, et al. The CNS connectome of a tadpole larva of Ciona intestinalis (L.) highlights sidedness in the brain of a chordate sibling[J]. Elife, 2016, 5: e16962."

The file elife-16962-fig16-data1-v1.xlsx contains the complete connectivity matrix for the entire brain of a larva of Ciona intestinalis. Additionally, the file elife-16962-fig3-all-neurons.xlsx provides a summary of all neurons present in the larval CNS of Ciona intestinalis.
